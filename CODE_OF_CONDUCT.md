# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We as contributors and maintainers of **HyperStreamKit** are committed to
providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

This project is developed and maintained by **cybernahid-dev** with the goal of
building a professional, respectful, and future-focused open-source community.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and considerate in discussions
- Providing constructive feedback
- Accepting criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Helping others learn and grow

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disrespecting maintainers or contributors

---

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate corrective action when needed
- Protecting the integrity and vision of the project

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not
align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the project maintainer.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated fairly and confidentially.

Appropriate actions may include:

- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent removal from the community

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Commit messages
- All project-related communication spaces

---

## Final Note

HyperStreamKit is built to shape the future of Android streaming.

Respect, professionalism, and collaboration are core to that mission.

Thank you for being part of this community.
